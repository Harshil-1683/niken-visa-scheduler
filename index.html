Common Questions

Everything you need to know before installing.

Why is it not on the Chrome Web Store? ▾
The extension was submitted and is under Google review (usually 3–7 days). While waiting, we provide this direct download so you don't have to wait. Once approved it will be on the official store.
How many free credits do I get? ▾
Every new user gets 1,000 free credits automatically on first install. At the default 30-second check frequency that's about 8+ hours of continuous monitoring — no payment needed to get started.
Is it safe to turn on Developer Mode? ▾
Yes — completely safe. Developer Mode is a built-in Chrome feature used by millions of developers. You can turn it off after installing and the extension keeps working.
Will it book a worse date by mistake? ▾
No — it only books dates earlier than your current appointment within the range you set. It will never book a later date under any circumstances.
Does it work on Mac and Windows? ▾
Yes! Works on any computer running Chrome, Edge, or Brave — Windows 10, Windows 11, or macOS.
